About Vanya Organics

Founded in 2022 in Jaipur, India, Vanya Organics is a homegrown brand focused on harnessing the potency of Indian botanicals — like turmeric, neem, and aloe — to create effective, gentle skincare. We partner with local farmers and use small-batch production to ensure quality and traceability.

  • Manufacturing: Rajasthan (ISO-certified facility)
  • Shipping: Pan-India with express options to major cities
  • Support: Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm IST
